Shipping, Return & Refund Policy

Shipping Policy

We offer free shipping on orders over RM100. Shipping times may vary depending on your location. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • International Shipping: 7-14 business days

Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition with all tags attached.

  • Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer.
  • Refunds will be processed once we receive the returned items.
  • Sale items are final and cannot be returned or exchanged.

Refund Policy

Refunds are issued to the original payment method once your return is received and inspected. Please allow 7-10 business days for the refund to be processed.

  • Refunds will include the cost of the item and any applicable taxes.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable.
  • If you received a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 7 days of receipt for a full refund or replacement.
