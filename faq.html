Frequently Asked Questions

What is Thrift Shop?

Thrift Shop is an online store that offers a wide variety of preloved items at affordable prices. Our inventory includes clothing, accessories, footwear, and more for both men and women.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our collections, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will need to create an account or log in if you already have one. Follow the steps to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal. All transactions are securely processed.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within Malaysia. We hope to expand our shipping options in the future to include international destinations.

What is your return policy?

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item within 14 days of receiving it. The item must be in its original condition and packaging. Please refer to our Shipping, Return & Refund Policy for more details.

How can I contact customer service?

If you need assistance, you can contact our customer service team via email at support@thriftshop.com or through our Customer Service page. We are here to help!
